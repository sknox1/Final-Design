My Hero Academia Characters:

Izuku Midoriya (Deku)

Voiced by Daiki Yamashita

Quirk: One For All A brave and determined young hero-in-training who inherited his power from the legendary All Might. Despite being born quirkless, Deku’s selflessness and tireless work ethic prove that true heroism comes from the heart.

Katsuki Bakugo (Kacchan)

Voiced by Clifford Chapin

Quirk: Explosion Fiercely competitive and incredibly powerful, Bakugo is Deku’s childhood rival with an explosive temper. Though aggressive, his unyielding drive to be the best makes him a true contender for the top hero spot.
